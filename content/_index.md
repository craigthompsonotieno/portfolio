I am a data scientist, data engineer, and researcher focused on transforming complex datasets into practical insights for decision-making in public health, infrastructure, and global development. My work combines statistical analysis, machine learning, data engineering, GIS, and visualization to support evidence-based decisions.


## What I Do

- **Data Analysis & Visualization**  
  Turning raw data into clear insights using Python, R, Power BI, and reproducible analytical workflows.

- **Data Engineering & Pipelines**  
  Building structured systems for data cleaning, matching, classification, validation, and reporting.

- **Survey & Statistical Analysis**  
  Analyzing household surveys, administrative data, and development indicators to support evidence-based policy.

- **GIS & Spatial Analytics**  
  Mapping geographic patterns in service delivery, infrastructure, risk, and development outcomes.

## Featured Projects

### Maji Ndogo Water Access Analysis

An end-to-end analysis of water access and infrastructure performance, identifying service delivery gaps and operational inefficiencies.

### Superstore Sales Performance Dashboard

An interactive Microsoft Excel dashboard project analyzing sales, profit, regional performance, category contribution, customer segments, discount impact, and profitability patterns using the Superstore dataset.

A survey-based analysis of maternal, newborn, child, and adolescent health indicators using national health data.

### World Bank Human Capital ML in Stata

A reproducible Stata 15.1 machine learning workflow using World Bank Development Indicators to explore, cluster, and predict survival to age 65 across country-year observations.

A data engineering project that classifies datasets according to Sustainable Development Goal themes using structured matching rules.
