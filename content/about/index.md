I am Craig Thompson Otieno, a statistician, data analyst, and data scientist based in Nairobi, Kenya.

I build data-driven solutions that transform raw and complex datasets into clean, structured, and decision-ready insights. My work spans survey analysis, geospatial mapping, automated reporting, dashboard development, and applied data science for public health, human rights, labour markets, and development policy.

## Background

I hold a Bachelor of Science in Statistics from Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology (JKUAT). I am currently pursuing the MITx MicroMasters in Statistics and Data Science through edX, with a focus on machine learning, fundamentals of statistics, and time series analysis.

My additional training includes a Data Science certification from ALX Africa, covering Python, SQL, AWS, and machine learning, as well as training in inequality analysis using Stata through the World Health Organization, focused on health equity and disaggregated survey data.
